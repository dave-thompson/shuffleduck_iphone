Deck Summary Screen

Touching any deck on the Library screen will show you a summary of that deck.

Aside from shuffling your cards, the Deck Summary screen allows you to do what you'll do most: either Learn, Test or Reference the deck.

Pay attention to the known counts at the top. ShuffleDuck remembers which cards you know and which you don't. In Learn mode, he will only give you cards you've not yet marked as known. In Test and Reference modes, you see both known and unknown cards.

The Deck ID shown here is also important. You can give it to your friends so that they can download this deck to their phones too.
