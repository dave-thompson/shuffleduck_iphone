Learn mode

In learn mode, ShuffleDuck only puts the first 10 unknown cards into your hand. You can flick through those 10, left or right. Try to remember the second side or just tap the screen to reveal it.

If you think you now know the current card, tap the tick button to turn it green and mark the card as known. You will not see this card again until you take a test.

Each time you 'know' (tick) a card, the friendly duck will take it off you and replace it with a new one for you to learn.

Once you know all the cards, you will be offered the opportunity to take a test. You can also start a test anytime from the Deck Summary screen.
